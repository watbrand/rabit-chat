- All data is encrypted in transit using TLS
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
- Sessions are secured with HTTP-only cookies
- Optional two-factor authentication available

#### Data Deletion
- Users can delete their account through Settings > Account > Delete Account
- Account deletion removes all personal data within 30 days
- Users can also request data export before deletion

## 5. AGE RATING & PARENTAL GUIDANCE

### Minimum Age Requirement
**13 years old**

### Parental Consent
- Users aged 13-17 are advised to have parental/guardian consent
- Terms of Service and EULA require parental consent for users under 18
- Parents can contact support@rabitchat.com for account inquiries

### Age Verification
- Users must confirm they are 13+ during registration
- Checkbox confirmation: "I confirm that I am 13 years or older"
- Terms of Service agreement required before account creation

### Why 13+?
RabitChat follows industry standards set by major social platforms:
- Instagram: 13+
- TikTok: 13+
- Facebook: 13+
- Snapchat: 13+
- Twitter/X: 13+

### Child Safety Features
- All content is moderated for policy violations
- Users can report inappropriate content
- Blocking feature available for unwanted interactions
- Anonymous gossip feature includes moderation controls
- No direct messaging from strangers without following

### COPPA Compliance
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
- If we discover a user is under 13, their account will be deleted
- Parents can request information about or deletion of their child's account
